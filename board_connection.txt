PCM connection (I2S):
- Data: GP4
- SCK:  GP5
- LRCK: GP6

LCD (SPI):
- BLK: 9
- SCL: 10
- SDA: 11
- CS:  13
- DC:  14
- RES: 15


See example: https://github.com/elehobica/pico_st7735_80x160

- Button Backward 18

- Button 2 20
